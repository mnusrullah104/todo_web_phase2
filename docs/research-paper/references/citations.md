# APA Citation Template

## Book Citation
Author, A. A. (Year). *Title of work: Capital letter also for subtitle*. Publisher.

## Journal Article Citation
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of article. *Title of Periodical, volume*(issue), pages. https://doi.org/xx.xxx/yyyy

## Online Source Citation
Author, A. A. (Year, Month Day). *Title of page*. Site Name. URL

## Multiple Authors (3+)
Author, A. A., Author, B. B., & Author, C. C. (Year). Title of work. *Journal Name, volume*(issue), pages.

## In-Text Citation Examples
- Single author: (Smith, 2020)
- Two authors: (Smith & Jones, 2020)
- Three or more authors: (Smith et al., 2020)
- Direct quote: (Smith, 2020, p. 15)

## Reference List Format
- Alphabetical order by author last name
- Hanging indent (second line indented)
- Double-spaced
- DOI or URL when available

## Notes
- Italicize book titles and journal names
- Use sentence case for article titles
- Include retrieval dates only for sources that may change
- For sources without DOI, include stable URL
